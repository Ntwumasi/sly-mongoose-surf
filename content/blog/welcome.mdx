## Welcome

Thanks for stopping by. This is where we'll share stories from the shaping bay, thoughts on surfboard design, and updates on what we're working on.

## What We're About

We shape surfboards because we love the craft. There's something special about taking raw materials and turning them into something that glides across a wave.

Whether it's a traditional surfboard, a finless alaia, a paipo for prone riding, or a handplane for bodysurfing—each piece carries the same intention: to help you connect with the ocean.

## Stay Tuned

We'll be sharing more here soon. In the meantime, feel free to reach out if you have questions or want to talk about a custom board.

See you in the water.
